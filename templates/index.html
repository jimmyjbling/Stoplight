Get Molecular Stoplight Properties From SMILES

This is an online web portal to calculate/estimate various chemical properties to help prioritize compounds during early stage drug discovery campaigns. Each property will be assigned a score based on predetermined cutoffs, and a total score will be calculated for each compound. Lower scores are better. The properties calculated and scoring cutoff are from Ackloo, S., Al-awar, R., Amaro, R.E. et al. CACHE (Critical Assessment of Computational Hit-finding Experiments): A public–private partnership benchmarking initiative to enable the development of computational methods for hit-finding. Nat Rev Chem 6, 287–295 (2022). https://doi.org/10.1038/s41570-022-00363-z. For more information on this approach please read refer to the 'CACHE Benchmarking' section of the paper

Please note that Solubility and LogP are estimated properties, thus can be inaccurate. Please take this into account when prioritizing compounds

Click here to see a table of score cutoffs for each property

When uploading a SMILES file make sure smiles are newline seperated (ei a csv with one column and each row is a SMILES). Uploaded files will auto-populate the text area, wait to generate csv until you see them in the box. CSV will be a row for each SMILES with columns for all active properties values, the total score and the score breakdown for each property. Score breakdown is ; seperated ei 1;0;2;1;0 means the first property was 1, second was 0...
